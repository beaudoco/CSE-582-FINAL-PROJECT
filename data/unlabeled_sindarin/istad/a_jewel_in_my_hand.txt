Mîr Mi Cammen
Gerin athan-dírad
i 'land gened lín;
ú-erin cened an ir
mistannech.
Gerin athan-nored
ínnath fíreb lín;
alui anirnen
le awarthad.
Iavais ar Ethuil
odog herain
gwanner nu gevennin
erainion.
Na dolthad lín
awarthannen nos nín
aphadol i râd
nan Annűn.
Na bith gwaith nín
estannech nin gwador.
Na bith gwaith lín
estannen le mellon.
Gernin iest aphaded
nan garas fain
a gernin iest aphaded
nan morannon.
Si derin ú-genel
a tiriel ú-nad
athan vîr gelevon
mi cammen.
Palan go-drevennim
a go-farnem:
dan mistannech na had
ú-'erin baded.
