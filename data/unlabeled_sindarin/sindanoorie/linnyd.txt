Linnod o Hithil
Glinga i Rawn o galað, blâb sui lasseg erui
Linnod o Hedraith
Panha i Derbad amarth, pathrathar ylf na-vedui.
Hi man i ylf aphathratha enni?
Linnod o Hestel Gýr
Tuia i gýron eden, harthad 'oduia 'lamui.
Linnod o Hereth
Minniel arðon ereb, gwanhathon os-san ereb.
Tâd Linnyd o Heblaer
Coe si nimminnen a moe, danhen i loss o thylys.
Danhen i loss o thylys, luithio rhîw i laer!
