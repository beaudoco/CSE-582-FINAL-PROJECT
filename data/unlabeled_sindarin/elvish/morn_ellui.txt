Morn 'ellui
A adan fael, lasto enni!,
i Nogoth ereb pent ef fuin. 
Im broniant ann-lend bangad mhegil gerthui echannen na nGelydh a thand lebethron togannen od eryn chaeron Ennorath.
Di-melegachas oh Yrch,
i machor 'enediant:
Neder miriain ani' naidh hin phain, egor leben an erui a canadh miriain, toloth cenaith, an edwen.
Glamhoth danc hon.
A adan fíreb, tiro nin!,
i Edhel ereb pent ef fuin.
Linnon i charthad uireb nin am meleth,
ant Belain melthin sogannen o ceilph gelebrin.
Glirint di-nguruthos:
Bartha men darthad ned Ennorath, tenn i nagor vedui o menel.
Glamhoth danc hon.
Nef ardhon, ben-edhel a ben-nogoth,
Balan ercheneb, crebain tad erin dhôl dîn,
lála:
Sí na vedui, gerin Gannas Ennorath mi-chammen angren.
Morgoth leithiannen.
